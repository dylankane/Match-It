How To Play

Click or tap on a card to flip it and see the hidden image. Then flip a second card to see if you can find the matching image.

Try to remember what image is on the back of each card as you flip them, once you have found a pair they will stay flipped.

Find as many pairs as you can in 1 minute, and try to complete it in a as few moves as possible.
